# Skill: Pipeline Health Check — Measurement & Diagnostics

## AI-Native Capability

## Purpose
Diagnose the health of the Client Attraction Engine pipeline, identify bottlenecks, provide benchmarks by industry, calculate ROI, and recommend specific fixes for underperforming stages.

## When to Use
- Pipeline is live but not producing expected results
- Want to understand which phase needs improvement
- Need benchmarks to know if your numbers are "good" or "bad"
- Calculating ROI on marketing spend
- Quarterly or monthly performance review
- Deciding where to invest next (which amplifier, which phase to optimize)

---

## The Pipeline Diagnostic Model

Every pipeline has 5 measurable stages. A weakness in ANY stage bottlenecks everything downstream.

```
TRAFFIC ──→ CAPTURE ──→ NURTURE ──→ CONVERSATION ──→ CLIENT
  │             │           │            │              │
  ▼             ▼           ▼            ▼              ▼
Visitors     Opt-ins     Engaged     Consultations    Sales
             (leads)    Subscribers    Booked         Closed
```

### The 5-Stage Metric Framework

| Stage | Key Metric | Formula | What It Tells You |
|-------|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **1. Traffic** | Visitors/month | Total unique visitors to Capture Point | Are enough people seeing your offer? |
| **2. Capture** | Opt-in Rate | (Opt-ins ÷ Visitors) × 100 | Is your landing page converting? |
| **3. Nurture** | Engagement Rate | (Email opens + clicks + replies) ÷ Subscribers | Is the Trust Ladder building relationship? |
| **4. Conversation** | Consultation Rate | (Consultations booked ÷ Active subscribers) × 100 | Are nurture subscribers becoming prospects? |
| **5. Close** | Close Rate | (Clients ÷ Consultations) × 100 | Are conversations becoming revenue? |

### The Master Metric: Cost Per Client (CPC)

```
Cost Per Client = Total Marketing Spend ÷ New Clients Acquired

Example:
  Ad spend:        $500/month
  Email tool:       $50/month
  Website:          $30/month
  Total spend:     $580/month
  New clients:     4/month
  Cost Per Client: $145

If average client value = $2,000 → ROI = 1,272%
```

| Meta Ads | 10-50 clicks/day at $10-15/day | Broader reach, lower intent |

| Social media | 20-200/month per active platform | Depends on audience size and engagement |
| Podcast appearances | 10-100 per episode | Highly qualified traffic |
| Direct mail | 1-5% response rate | 10-50 responses per 1,000 mailed |

### Capture Benchmarks (Landing Page Opt-in Rate)
| Rating | Opt-in Rate | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Excellent | 40%+ | Scale traffic — your page converts well |
| Good | 25-40% | Test headline variants to push higher |
| Average | 15-25% | Redesign copy using PHA framework, test elements |
| Poor | Under 15% | Major overhaul needed — value proposition or targeting issue |

### Nurture Benchmarks (Email Engagement)
| Metric | Healthy | Concerning |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Open rate | 25-45% | Under 15% |
| Click rate | 3-7% | Under 1% |
| Reply rate | 1-3% | 0% (nobody is engaging) |
| Unsubscribe rate | Under 0.5% per email | Over 1% per email |
| Spam complaints | Under 0.1% | Over 0.3% (deliverability risk) |

### Conversation Benchmarks
| Type | Healthy Rate | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free consultation requests | 2-5% of subscribers over time | From Trust Ladder email #7 |
| Paid consultation bookings | 1-3% of subscribers over time | Higher quality, lower volume |
| Webinar attendee → consultation | 10-25% | Warm leads |

### Close Benchmarks
| Context | Healthy Close Rate | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| Free consultation → client | 15-25% | Higher if Trust Ladder is strong |
| Paid consultation → client | 40-65% | Self-selecting for seriousness |
| Webinar → direct purchase | 5-15% of attendees | Depends on price point |

## Bottleneck Diagnosis

### "I'm Getting Traffic But No Opt-ins"
**Problem**: Capture Point (Phase 3)
**Likely Causes:**
- Headline doesn't match ad message (message mismatch)
- Value Magnet doesn't feel valuable enough
- Too much friction (too many form fields, slow page, confusing layout)
- Not mobile-optimized
- Targeting wrong audience (ad brings unqualified visitors)

**Fixes:**
1. Run A/B headline test (Split Test Lab)
2. Simplify the form (first name + email only)
3. Add a video to the page
4. Verify mobile experience
5. Check ad targeting alignment

### "I'm Getting Opt-ins But No Engagement"
**Problem**: Trust Ladder (Phase 4)
**Likely Causes:**
- Emails landing in spam/promotions tab
- Subject lines aren't compelling
- Content doesn't match what was promised
- Emails feel corporate, not personal
- Sending too frequently or too infrequently

**Fixes:**
1. Check deliverability (use mail-tester.com)
2. A/B test subject lines
3. Rewrite emails in a more conversational tone
4. Ask subscribers to whitelist your email
5. Adjust frequency (test every 2-3 days vs. every 5-7 days)

### "I'm Getting Engagement But No Consultation Requests"
**Problem**: Trust Ladder → Conversation transition
**Likely Causes:**
- Consultation offer isn't compelling enough
- Fear of sales pressure
- Unclear what the consultation covers
- Too early in the sequence (trust not yet established)
- No follow-up after sequence ends

**Fixes:**
1. Reframe consultation as "assessment" or "strategy session" with clear deliverable
2. Add more proof/case studies before the offer
3. Extend sequence (add emails 8-10 before the offer)
4. Offer a paid consultation with guarantee (filters for serious prospects)
5. Add ongoing broadcast emails after sequence ends

### "I'm Getting Consultations But Not Closing"
**Problem**: Sales process (beyond the pipeline)
**Likely Causes:**
- Unqualified leads (pipeline is attracting the wrong people)
- Price/value mismatch
- No clear next step during the consultation
- Trying to close too quickly (or too slowly)
- No follow-up after the consultation

**Fixes:**
1. Add qualification questions to the booking form
2. Review whether the niche targeting is correct (Phase 1)
3. Create a structured consultation framework (diagnose, prescribe, propose)
4. Add a post-consultation follow-up sequence (3 emails over 5 days)
5. Consider paid consultations to pre-qualify

## ROI Calculation Templates

### Simple ROI
```
ROI = ((Revenue from New Clients - Marketing Cost) ÷ Marketing Cost) × 100

Example:
  Revenue from 4 new clients @ $2,000 each = $8,000
  Marketing cost: $580
  ROI = (($8,000 - $580) ÷ $580) × 100 = 1,279%
```

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) ROI
```
CLV = Average Revenue Per Client × Average Client Lifespan (in months/years)

CLV-Adjusted ROI = ((CLV × New Clients - Marketing Cost) ÷ Marketing Cost) × 100

Example:
  Average revenue per client: $2,000/year
  Average client stays: 3 years
  CLV: $6,000
  4 new clients × $6,000 = $24,000 lifetime value
  Marketing cost: $580/month × 12 = $6,960/year
  CLV-Adjusted ROI = (($24,000 - $6,960) ÷ $6,960) × 100 = 245%
```

### Break-Even Analysis
```
Break-Even Point = Marketing Cost ÷ Revenue Per Client

Example:
  Monthly marketing cost: $580
  Revenue per client: $2,000
  Break-even: 0.29 clients/month (less than 1 client pays for everything)
```
